About Us

Facilitating Entrepreneurship

E-Cell IIT ROPAR of TBIF (Technology Business Incubation Foundation) is a non-profit organisation managed by a team of students. We develop entrepreneurial culture through hand-holding of ventures by encouraging & mentoring them and make their endeavour worthwhile.
We explore all the dimensions of the entrepreneurial world by conducting various kinds of events like Workshops to enrich the skills, highly engaging Bootcamps, interactive speaker sessions and competitions to challenge them throughout the year. We provide ample opportunities to bring students, startups, alumni and corporates all together.

Mission

Creating the Creators

The Entrepreneurship Cell IIT Ropar focusses on the whole journey of enthusiastic young entrepreneurs from the idea generation to the successful sustainable Enterprise. E-Cell aims to unlatch the hidden potential of students and guide them to be successful entrepreneurs.
